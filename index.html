Welcome To Puzzle Game 2048

This is a single-player sliding tile puzzle game. In this game you can choose one of two type of games are available. The available games is 2048 and 3072. To win this game, you need to combine the numbers to reach the 2048 and 3072 tile depend on the type of games you choose. This game is compatible on desktop and mobile device.
NOTED: 2048 was created by Gabriele Cirulli and was modified into 3072 by Joshua Dombrowsky.
